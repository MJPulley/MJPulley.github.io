    • Hello Residents of Prince George's County

    Are you tired of wondering where all of the speed cameras are in the county?

    As well as getting snapped by an unsuspecting speed camera?

    Well, our team has created a visualization for users like you to get an understanding of the placement of certain speed cameras, and have an appreciation for their purpose and placement around the area.

    This map gives an approximate location for each speed camera in PG county. By hitting the "Refresh" button, it presents new markers on the map, which you can click on and see the address of where the speed camera is located, as well as the posted speed for that specific camera.

    Take a look and search around and see how many you can find where!
